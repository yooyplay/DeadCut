Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1

Your videos never leave your device

DeadCut does not upload, transmit, or store your video files. The Mac app processes everything locally using ffmpeg on your machine. The browser tool detects and cuts videos entirely within your browser using WebAssembly. No file ever reaches a server.

2

No analytics or tracking

DeadCut does not use Google Analytics, ad networks, or any third-party tracking on this website. No usage data is collected.

3

No accounts, no personal data

DeadCut has no signup, no login, and collects no personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details. We have no user database.

4

Contact form

If you use the contact form, your name, email, and message are sent to us via FormSubmit.co. We use this information only to respond to your message and do not share it with third parties.

5

Third-party links

This site links to Ko-fi. That service has its own privacy policy which we do not control.

6

Contact

Questions? Use our contact form.
